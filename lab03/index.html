Spaghetti with Meat Sauce

The simplest and tastiest spaghetti recipe you'll find.

This is my personal favorite recipe because it's so easy and delicious to make when you're hungry and just want something quick and simple to cook.

Ingredients

For this recipe you'll need:

  • 1lb Ground beef
  • 1 jar of Meat flavored Prego sauce (or whichever flavor you prefer)
  • Half a white onion
  • Salt & pepper
  • Oregano
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Chop the onion in dices.
  2. Bring a pot of water to a boil and add salt.
  3. Add pasta noodles to boiling water and boil until al dente.
  4. Heat up a skillet with a drizzle of olive oil to medium heat.
  5. Add ground beef to heated skillet and chop it up.
  6. Once meat is almost fully browned, add in diced onion.
  7. When the meat and onions are fully browned, add in Prego sauce.
  8. Add salt, pepper, and oregano into the meat sauce (along with whatever seasonings you like).
  9. Simmer meat sauce on low heat for at least 15 minutes.
  10. Strain pasta noodles.
  11. (Optional) Add pasta noodles into meat sauce and mix in.
  12. Serve and enjoy!
