Subary Legacy

Subaru Legacy
Average price $8,000-11,000
Rec. model years 2013+

This is a less common, but underrated car for teens or new drivers. The Subaru Legacy is a sedan with AWD making it well suited for various weather conditions. They are decently reliable and are cheaper to fill up with gas than a lot of cars, costing no more than $45 to completely fill the tank from empty.
